Meredith

A Winning Platform Strategy for a Leading Publisher.

Corporate websites too often take the form of a monologue, filled with dry data and stale, unchanging content - an experience that the Meredith Corporation wanted to steer away from. With an audience of over 100 million women Meredith is clearly a category leader, a fact that their digital presence needed to reflect more strongly.

Working closely with Meredith's CMO, DOMANI developed a dynamic, contemporary design elevating the Meredith brand and helping ensure that each visitor walk away from the site with a better understanding of Meredith's offerings and a deeper connection to the lifestyles reflected within their broad range of publications.

Best-in-class gets an upgrade.

Built on the Drupal framework Meredith’s new digital presence speaks to advertisers, prospective and current employees, and investors who arrive at the site with a wide range of backgrounds and interests. The new Meredith.com facilitates these many levels of conversation without requiring a crippling amount of administration or maintenance. Visitors can easily navigate between the local and national media brands, investors can locate all relevant stock and company news, and job seekers can get a deeper look at what it means to become a part of the Meredith family.

The revamped site features a responsive design framework fully optimized for mobile users on every device and screen size, catering to the on-the-go lifestyle of Meredith's target audience. By incorporating an intuitive navigation scheme with clean page designs, DOMANI created a site that serves as a jumping-off point for their readership to quickly connect with their favorite Meredith publications and as the central point for conversation about the company.

  • Meredith's brand platform transcends the average corporate site with a fresh, dynamic design
  • Responsive framework serves visitors on every device and screen size
  • Live feed displays the latest news and social content across Meredith's many brands
